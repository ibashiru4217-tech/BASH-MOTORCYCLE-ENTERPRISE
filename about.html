About BASH ENTERPRISE

A team of riders and mechanics dedicated to keeping Nigeria's motorcycles bought, sold and running right.

Who We Are

Motorcycles Are Our Trade, Trust Is Our Business

BASH ENTERPRISE was founded in 2025 to solve a simple problem: riders needed a dealer and workshop they could rely on for straight answers, fair prices and work done right the first time.

Located at Ang Mall Yaro along Grave Yard Road, we buy, sell and repair motorcycles of every make, backed by a small team that treats each bike with the same care we'd want for our own.

What started as a commitment to honest dealing has grown into a full-service stop for buying, selling, servicing and sourcing genuine spare parts — all under one roof.
